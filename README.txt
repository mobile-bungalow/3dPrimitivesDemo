USE:

	-Drag and drop Driver.html into any browser that can use webGL
	-left click to add points to the cylinder chain.
	-right click to finish an object
	-click a cylinder object to select it, click it again to deselect
	-Drag with right click to rotate and object
	-Drag with left click to reposition it
	-use the buttons to toggle specular and smooth lighting
	
Notes:
	-Much of the math that could be in the shaders is exposed for educational purposes
	-As a result it is far less extensible than it could be
